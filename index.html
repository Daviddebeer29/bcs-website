What We Do

Our Services

From industrial automation to solar energy and security — we deliver end-to-end solutions for commercial, industrial and residential clients across the Western Cape.

Machine Automation

We design, build and commission industrial control systems to improve efficiency, reduce downtime and increase productivity across your operations.

  • PLC Programming & Commissioning
  • Industrial Control Panel Design & Build
  • Motor Control & Drive Systems
  • Automation Integration & Maintenance

Solar Installations

Complete solar power systems designed and installed to reduce your electricity costs and provide reliable backup power during outages.

  • Ground-Mounted & Roof Panel Arrays
  • Inverter & Battery Backup Systems
  • System Design & Commissioning
  • Energy-Saving Solar Solutions

CCTV Installation

Advanced surveillance systems installed with precision for maximum coverage, reliability and peace of mind — for estates, schools and businesses.

  • Estate & Perimeter Security
  • Indoor & Outdoor Camera Systems
  • Advanced CCTV Technology
  • Maintenance & Upgrades

Electrical Services

A full range of professional electrical services carried out to industry safety standards — for residential, commercial and industrial clients.

  • Installations & Wiring
  • DB Board Upgrades & Compliance
  • Fault Finding & Maintenance
  • Electrical Upgrades

Panel Building

Custom electrical panel design, assembly and installation for industrial, commercial and residential applications — built to the highest safety and performance standards.

  • Custom Panel Design & Assembly
  • Distribution Board Wiring
  • Switchgear & Protection Systems
  • Compliance & Certification

Motor Control

Specialised motor control and variable speed drive solutions that reduce energy consumption, extend equipment life and optimise process performance.

  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
  • Soft Starters & Motor Protection
  • Motor Control Centres (MCCs)
  • Energy Optimisation & Monitoring
